Ethni-Connect FAQs

Please click Part 1 and Part 2 to read the details


Q1. What is Ethni-Connect?

A: Ethni-connect is a user-friendly, quick search platform that provides home-cooked food based on ethnicity.


Q2. What can a registered chef do on Ethni-Connect?

A: A chef can create their weekly menu and display the personalized dishes for customers to purchase.


Q3. Is there a standard quantity that needs to be served?

A: Yes. Detailed information will be shared as part of the on-boarding process.


Q4. Where is the Ethni-Connect office located?

A: Ethni-Connect is designed, developed and conceptualized by students at Oakland university having its head office in Rochester Hills.


Q5. How can I contact Ethni-Connect?

A: You can always reach us to ethniconnect@gmail.com and we will get back to you within 24 hours.


Q6. When will my profile go live?

A: The complete process for a chef profile to go live with their products listed in their area will be 7 to 10 Business days. This completely depends on how quick the profile is completed by the chef and coordination with our chef on-boarding team.

Q1. How can a chef choose the dishes / menu?

A: You can create a list of products that you wish to sell on Ethni-Connect.


Q2. What is a customer request?

A: Ideally, when a customer visits Ethni-connect he or she goes ahead and follows simple steps: Enter Zipcode, select a chef of their choice, check their menu and place an order.


Q3. Can I cook only on Weekdays / Weekends or select the days I want to cook?

A: Yes. When a user finds your listing and contacts you, please make sure to keep them informed on the days you service is active and more importantly keep them informed with prior notice for days that you will not be providing the service.


Q4. What can a registered chef do on Ethni-Connect?

A: A chef can list dishes for purchase & receive custom requests in their respective city and area.


Q5. How is the food packed?

A: Chefs can choose to procure packaging material / containers on their own. If you prefer to have any please add in the special instruction in view your order page.


Q6. How can we decide the cost per dish?

A: Cost per dish will depend on your input cost in making that dish plus your service fee.


Q7. Who delivers the food?

A: Chefs will be responsible to coordinate with the customer directly for organizing delivery or pick up of the food in case of an order.


Q8. How will Ethni-Connect help a chef get customers?

A: Promotions / Marketing campaigns run by Ethni-Connect and its channel partners, high service quality, good food, word-of-mouth and a little bit of chef’s effort will help the chef get orders.


Q9. Is there any contract that Chef’s need to sign?

A: A chef will have to acknowledge and accept our Chef Terms of Use as part of the registration process. A contract that governs terms & conditions such as commissions, payments cycle, acceptance of orders, on time food preparations and other terms will be given to the chef for careful understanding before signing.


Q10. When will a chef get their first order?

A: This depends on a lot of factors like the customer’s taste and preferences, the dishes listed by the chef, its availability, the pricing, ratings & reviews of the chef profile. As customers always look for good ratings & reviews before placing an order, participating in custom requests is the best way to build your profile.


Q11. What defines a “Meal”?

A: The food was served and eaten in one sitting. Detailed information will be shared as part of the on-boarding process.
